Adult Classes

Classes & Studio Rental Opportunities students workingOur classes are a great way to explore your creative side and have a good time. We invite you to join us for the fun! The tuition cost of all classes at the Clay Studio of Missoula includes: one 25 lb. bag of clay, use of studio facilities at all times (24/7--except the hours when other classes or special events are in session - see the current session calendar), and a personal storage cubby. Firing fees and extra clay cost an additional, nominal fee (unless otherwise noted). Call today to enroll (406-543-0509) or enroll online now!

RAKU - new 5 week class starts 10/17

Some experience in ceramics is helpful, but not required
Tuesday afternoons: October 17-November 14, 1-4pm (10 weeks)
Instructor: Dudley Anderson
$140 for members / $150 for non-members

Students will have the chance to work on projects independently in and outside of class and learn how to make forms that work effectively with raku, horsehair, and naked raku. The first class will involve an introduction to making works specifically for the raku kiln, which will be continued to be made and fired during the following sessions as work is completed and bisqued. CALL 543-0509 to register, or register online now!

Monday afternoons: September 11-November 14, 1-4pm (10 weeks)

Beginning Pottery

This class is suitable for absolute beginners and students needing a refresher
Enroll on one of the following 10-week sessions:
Monday evenings: September 11-November 13, 6-9pm ***Monday sesssion is FULL***
(accepting waitlist requests - call or email us to be placed on the waitlist)
Instructor: Kirk Jackson, Artist in Residence
OR
Thursday evenings: September 14-November 16, 6-9pm
Instructor: Andrew Avakian, Artist in Residence
$210 for members / $225 for non-members
Students will learn about clay bodies, wheel-throwing, and hand building techniques, as well as processes for glazing and firing. Participants will practice making a variety of ceramic forms including cups, handled mugs, bowls, vases, and plates. Requirements: A positive attitude and desire to try and learn!

Bring your ideas to create your own projects. Weekly demonstrations will include new ideas and techniques, as well as demos designed to suit the needs of students enrolled. CALL 543-0509 to register, or register online now!

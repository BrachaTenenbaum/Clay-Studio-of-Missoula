Private Lessons, Parties or Tours

Scheduling

Tours must be scheduled at least 1 week ahead of time. Classes, workshops, and parties must be scheduled at least 3 weeks ahead of time. Available times vary and are contingent upon staffing and time availability.

Tours

All tours are free! Tours last approximately ½ hour and can be tailored to fit any age group or area of interest.

Potential Themes: 1. All About The Clay Studio- History, Facilities, Opportunities 2. Gallery and Ceramic Art- gallery tour, facilities, resident studios 3. Clay 101- making wet to fired work Add a wheel demo- $15 (A wheel demonstration adds up to ½ hour to the tour and must be arranged ahead of time.) You can schedule a kids clay project in conjunction with your tour- call for details and rates 406-543-0509

Parties

Includes Kids Birthday Parties, Adult/Organization Parties, and One Time Group Lessons Clay Studio parties last 2 hours and focus on a hand-building project. Throwing projects available upon special request for ages 8 and up. Parties include instruction and materials. Finished projects are fired and available for pickup approximately 2-3 weeks after the event Rates: $100 total for up to 10 people ($125 for wheel throwing project) $10 per additional participant

Private Lessons

Depending on instructor availability, we are able to offer pre-scheduled private lessons. Please contact our Outreach Coordinator, Jazmine Raymond, for details and rates fire@theclaystudioofmissoula.org
